# Copywriting Prompt — Cat Charity Poland

> Run after reading product-marketing-context.md

---

## Your Task

Write all marketing copy for a Polish cat adoption charity website. All copy must be in **Polish**. The tone is warm, direct, honest — never guilt-tripping, never clichéd.

---

## Page-by-Page Copy to Write

### 1. Strona Główna — Hero Section

Write 3 headline variants + subheadline + primary CTA button text + secondary CTA.

Requirements:
- Headline: max 8 words. Emotionally resonant but not manipulative. Should speak to the adopter's desire, not the cat's suffering.
- Subheadline: 1–2 sentences. Explain what we do and who we are.
- Primary CTA: action-oriented, warm ("Poznaj nasze koty", not "Kliknij tutaj")
- Secondary CTA: lower commitment ("Dowiedz się więcej")

Test your headlines against this: would a real Warsaw resident in their 30s roll their eyes at it, or feel something genuine?

Good examples to draw from:
- "Każdy kot ma charakter. Znajdź swój."
- "Adopcja zaczyna się od jednego spojrzenia."
- "Nie szukamy idealnych domów. Szukamy prawdziwych."

---

### 2. Sekcja Statystyki (homepage)

Write 3–4 stats with short emotional labels. Numbers are placeholders — write the label copy.

Format: [LICZBA] + [krótki label 3–5 słów]
Examples: "342 — kotów znalazło dom", "12 lat — pomagamy bezdomnym kotom"

Write labels that make numbers feel human, not like an annual report.

---

### 3. Sekcja "Jak adoptować" (homepage + /adoptuj)

Write a 4-step adoption process description. Each step:
- Short bold title (3–5 words)
- 1–2 sentence description
- Warm, not bureaucratic

The Polish adoption context: many people fear being rejected or judged. Address this fear directly in at least one step. Reference that we ask questions to find the right match, not to gatekeep.

---

### 4. Profil Kota — Template

Write a reusable template for individual cat profile pages. Include:

**Section A: Imię kota — the opening hook (2–3 sentences)**
This is the most important copy on the page. It must capture the cat's personality in a way that helps the right person self-select. Not: "Misia szuka kochającego domu." Yes: "Misia potrzebuje czasu. Pierwszego tygodnia będzie się chować. Drugiego — przyjdzie sama."

**Section B: Charakter (4–6 bullet traits)**
Honest, specific. Include at least one thing that is "not for everyone." Examples:
- "Lubi siedzieć obok, nie na kolanach"
- "Nie przepada za dziećmi poniżej 10 lat"
- "Głośno mruczy kiedy jest głodna — o 5 rano też"

**Section C: Historia (1 paragraph)**
Where did this cat come from. Brief, factual, emotionally honest. Don't over-dramatise.

**Section D: Szuka domu gdzie... (3–4 bullet requirements)**
What kind of home/adopter this cat actually needs. Be specific. "Spokojna atmosfera" is useless. "Dom bez innych kotów, przynajmniej na początku" is useful.

**Section E: CTA**
Button: primary + secondary.
Primary: "Chcę adoptować [imię]"
Secondary: "Napisz do nas w sprawie [imię]"

---

### 5. Strona /wspieraj — Donate Page

Write copy for three giving options:

**A) Jednorazowa darowizna**
Short, warm. Explain what different amounts do (placeholder: "Za [kwota] możemy...").

**B) Adopcja wirtualna (miesięczna)**
Explain the concept: regular donation assigned to a specific cat, with updates. This is a high-conversion model used by Koty z Grochowa. Write the pitch.

**C) 1.5% podatku — this is the biggest CTA**
This is zero cost to the donor — they redirect 1.5% of already-owed tax. Many Poles don't know they can do this or forget. Write:
- A headline that makes this feel unmissable
- 2–3 sentences explaining it simply
- The exact instruction (fill in KRS number in annual tax return)
- A sense of scale: "Jeśli 100 osób przekaże 1,5%, to..."

---

### 6. Sekcja "Historie adopcji" (success stories)

Write a template for adoption success stories. Format:
- Photo caption style header: "[Imię kota] — teraz mieszka z [imię adoptera], [miasto]"
- 3–4 sentences from the adopter's perspective (first person)
- 1 sentence from the charity's perspective (optional)
- Hashtag suggestions for social sharing

Tone: real, slightly imperfect, not a press release. "Na początku się bałam, że sobie nie poradze. Ale Zuzia od razu wiedziała, że to jej dom."

---

### 7. /domy-tymczasowe — Foster Programme Page

Many potential adopters don't know fostering is an option. Write:
- A headline explaining what fostering is (don't assume people know)
- Why fostering matters (cats in real homes are easier to match with adopters)
- What's required of a foster carer (honest, not demanding)
- What the charity provides (food, vet costs, support)
- CTA: "Zostań domem tymczasowym"

---

## Tone Reminders

- Polish, second person singular ("Ty"), warm but not infantile
- No guilt-tripping
- No clichés ("daj mu szansę na miłość", "razem możemy więcej")
- Be honest about cats — they're not all cuddly, and that's fine
- Use specificity over generality always
- Short sentences. One idea per sentence.
